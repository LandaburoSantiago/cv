# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

This portfolio serves a mixed audience: recruiters evaluating hiring fit, tech leaders assessing technical judgment, and founders or clients looking for someone who can build product-facing software with business context. They typically arrive with limited time and want to understand credibility, range, and working style quickly.

## Product Purpose

This site exists to turn Santiago's experience into trust. It should make his profile feel clear, credible, and memorable, showing not just what technologies he has touched but how he thinks, executes, and contributes to digital products end-to-end. Success means visitors can understand his value fast, feel a personal point of view, and have an obvious next step to contact or download the CV.

## Brand Personality

Warm, creative, product-minded. The voice should feel direct, thoughtful, and grounded in real delivery work. It should communicate technical confidence without sounding cold, inflated, or overly corporate.

## Anti-references

- Generic dark SaaS aesthetics with blue glow, floating panels, and interchangeable startup visuals.
- Portfolio treatments that feel like a polished template rather than a personal point of view.
- Empty minimalism that removes personality and specificity.
- Decorative futurism that competes with the actual content.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with judgment, not just credentials.
2. Make the person feel present, not templated.
3. Show product thinking alongside technical depth.
4. Keep the path from first impression to contact frictionless.
5. Preserve clarity in both Spanish and English.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA as the baseline. Keep contrast solid, focus states visible, and motion optional or subdued for people who prefer reduced motion. Preserve clarity and consistency across both Spanish and English content so neither language feels secondary.
